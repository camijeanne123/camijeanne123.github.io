Site Purpose

Have you ever wondered what the weather was like where your grandparents live? Or do you need to know what to wear to your son's soccer game? Maybe you're going camping and you need to know if you should bring an extra tarp for the tent? Weather You Care Or Not is a up-to-date website that provides you with the weather, temperature, wind speed and direction, and much more. Weather You Care Or Not has a goal to provide you with the most updated weather patterns in the area of your choice.
