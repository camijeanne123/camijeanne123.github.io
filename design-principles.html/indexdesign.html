Hick's Law

Amazon.com

Amazon shows a great example of Hick's law. There are so many different products and categories. Hick's law allows them to focus on the search bar and the menu on the left for navigating through all the products and categories. Without it there would be hundreds of sheets just listing all the different products and categories randomly inefficiently.

Rule of Thirds

FoodNetwork.com

FoodNetwork really draws attention to their logo and the recipes that they are showing by using the rule of thirds. You can see that below the add, you see the logo for the website and then below at around 2/3 of the page you see a picture of the dish for the recipe that is being shown. This really catches your eye and has you focus on their key points on the page.

White Space and Clean Design

byui.edu

BYUI is the A+ student of white space and clean design. If you just look at the centering of the log-in area and the borders and white space around it, it just looks clean and simple. They also used colors that allow for clean design.
